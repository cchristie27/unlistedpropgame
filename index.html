Official Rules

No purchase necessary to enter or win
1. Sponsor

UNLISTED Daily Prop Game. Metro Detroit, Michigan. Contact: prizes@unlistedprop.com

2. Eligibility

Open to legal U.S. residents 18 or older. Void where prohibited. Sponsor employees ineligible.

3. Entry period

One prop per calendar day. Entry opens when the prop is posted and closes when the result is marked. One (1) entry per person per day. Duplicate emails are disqualified.

4. How to enter

Visit unlistedprop.com, submit your exact answer, and provide your name and email. No purchase required.

5. Winner selection

The entrant who submits the first chronologically correct, exact answer wins. Ties go to the earliest server timestamp. If no exact answer is submitted, no prize is awarded that day.

6. Prize

A $50 Amazon.com digital gift card delivered by email within 24 hours of result. Non-transferable. No cash equivalent.

7. Notification

Winner notified by email within 24 hours. Must respond within 72 hours or prize is forfeited.

8. Odds

Depend on total entries and number of exact correct answers. Historical rates on the History tab.

9. Privacy

Name and email used only to administer this promotion and notify winners. Not sold or shared.

10. Governing law

State of Michigan. Last updated June 2026.
